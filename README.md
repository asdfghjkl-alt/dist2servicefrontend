About distance2home.info
distance2home is a free Covid compliance checking tool. By entering your home address, distance2home will show

    your distance to home in green (compliant) or red (noncompliant)
    a compliance circleradius around your home. You may set the radius of the compliance circle to 10km, 5km, 2km, or 1km according to your local restriction rules.
